What is Expense Tracker ?

 
        An expense tracker is a tool or application designed to help individuals, households, or businesses monitor 
        and manage their financial expenditures. By systematically recording and categorizing expenses, these trackers 
        offer a clear view of where money is being spent and help users make more informed financial decisions.
        
        The primary goal of an expense tracker is to give users a detailed insight into their spending habits, 
        allowing them to identify areas where they might be overspending or where they can cut costs. 
        Whether you are trying to stick to a monthly budget, save for a big purchase, or simply become more 
        aware of your financial habits, an expense tracker is an invaluable tool.

Key Features of Expense Trackers:

Expense Input:
Users can log their daily, weekly, or monthly expenses, including details like the amount, category (e.g., food, housing, entertainment), and payment method.

Categorization:
Expenses are organized into categories (like transportation, food, bills), helping users understand where their money goes.

Budgeting:
Many expense trackers allow users to set spending limits for different categories and compare actual spending against budgeted amounts.

Visualization:
Most trackers offer graphs and charts that visually represent spending patterns, making it easier to identify trends and areas for improvement.

Reports:
Expense trackers often generate detailed reports, summarizing spending over a certain period (weekly, monthly, or yearly).

Reminders:
Some trackers can send alerts for upcoming bills or remind users to record their expenses regularly.
